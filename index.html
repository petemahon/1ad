Welcome to 1st Abu Dhabi

1st Abu Dhabi Scouts provides fun and friendship, challenge and everyday adventure to over 80 young people in Abu Dhabi, for both boys and girls aged 6 to 18.

Our Sections

There is a place in Scouts for almost all ages, either as a member of one of the sections below, or as a volunteer. Clicking the Learn More buttons below will take you to the UK Scouts website for further information.

All sections meet on Thursday evenings, during BSAK term time.
