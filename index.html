Welcome to Abu Dhabi Scouts

Abu Dhabi Scouts provides fun and friendship, challenge and everyday adventure to over 100 young people in Abu Dhabi, for both boys and girls aged 6 to 18.

Joining us couldn't be easier

Register your interest to join Abu Dhabi Scouts. We welcome everyone to join us, and will give priority to young people who have a parent willing to volunteer some of their time to help out. No previous experience necessary!

Join Abu Dhabi Scouts

For each young person you would like to register, fill out an entry form below. Adults above the age of 18 are welcome to volunteer and do not need to have a child in Scouting.
